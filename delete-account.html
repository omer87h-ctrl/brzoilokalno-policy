Delete Account and Data Request

App: Brzo i Lokalno
Contact: omer.creating.apps87@gmail.com

Users of Brzo i Lokalno can request deletion of their app account and associated data through the app or through this public web resource.

Option 1 - In-app deletion

Open the app and go to:

Settings > Delete Account

When available and technically possible, this process attempts to delete the account and associated account data directly from the app.

Option 2 - Web / email deletion request

If you no longer have access to the app, send a deletion request to:

omer.creating.apps87@gmail.com

Please include the following:

  1. The email address used for your Brzo i Lokalno account.
  2. A short statement that you want your app account and associated data deleted.
  3. Any identifying details that may help us locate your account, if needed.

What we delete

Upon a valid deletion request, we attempt to delete or remove associated account data such as:

  • account profile data
  • works and uploaded work images
  • job posts
  • offers and applications connected to the account
  • ordinary messages connected to the account
  • block records related to the account
  • other account-linked content where applicable

What may be retained

Some limited information may be retained, minimized, or anonymized where necessary for legitimate reasons such as security, fraud prevention, abuse handling, legal compliance, dispute handling, or record integrity.

For example, some rating-related records may be anonymized rather than kept linked to the deleted account.

Third-party systems note

Brzo i Lokalno uses third-party infrastructure (including Google/Firebase services). Some technical traces may remain for limited periods in backups, logs, or legal/security records according to provider and legal requirements, even after account deletion is processed.

Processing time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within a reasonable period. Additional time may be required where verification, technical review, or legal retention obligations apply.
